ABOUT US

Preventio Indonesia is a startup focused on developing advanced information technology-based healthcare products. Preventio Indonesia was founded by Margareth S. C. Simandjuntak (CEO), Bernand J. H. Simatupang (CFO), Pray P. H. Nadeak (CTO), and Fajar M. Y. Zain (CMO) in 2025 in Bandung City. Preventio Indonesia was created to address the challenge of providing smart preventive healthcare with affordable price


Vision:
Providing the most trusted AI & blockchain-based global digital healthcare ecosystem, enabling early detection of various diseases and providing more inclusive and secure access to healthcare.

Mission:
1. Developing trusted AI solutions for reading health indicators from visual and biometric data.
2. Building a blockchain ecosystem to ensure the security, privacy, and integrity of medical data.
3. Integrating tokenization ($PEVIO) to encourage engagement, incentives, and ecosystem sustainability.
4. Educating the public to be more aware of preventive health through easily accessible technology.
5. Collaborating with medical professionals, healthcare institutions, and regulators to maintain validity and compliance.

Core Values:
1. Innovation First → Focus on research and the latest technological solutions.
2. Preventive Mindset → Act before it's too late, don't just react.
3. Trust & Transparency → Blockchain as the basis for medical data security..
4. Accessibility → Digital health must be accessible to all.
5. Collaboration → Work together with doctors, hospitals, and communities.
6. Sustainability → Build a long-term ecosystem, including tokenization.



MODICHAIN



Our most recommended product is ModiChain (Mobile Detection based on AI and Blockhchain). ModiChain is a mobile-based solution designed for the early detection and prevention of cardiac health issues. The application uses AI and blockchain technology to provide its services. It analyzes a smartphone camera's video stream and user-provided information from a screening questionnaire. This data is then processed by a predictive AI/ML model to deliver real-time, interactive, and highly accurate health insights. Users can also receive personalized health nudges from an AI chatbot. The solution is built for data security and uses blockchain to offer incentive rewards. ModiChain is positioned in a "blue ocean" market, where it is both affordable and easy to access compared to its competitors.
