ABOUT US

Preventio Indonesia is a startup focused on developing advanced information technology-based healthcare products. Preventio Indonesia was founded by Margareth S. C. Simandjuntak (CEO), Bernand J. H. Simatupang (CFO), Pray P. H. Nadeak (CTO), and Fajar M. Y. Zain (CMO) in 2025 in Bandung City. Preventio Indonesia was created to address the challenge of providing smart preventive healthcare at an affordable price, tackling the pressing issue of cardiac health in Indonesia.

Indonesia faces a cardiac health emergency. Heart attack cases are a top killer, with 20 million cases reported in 2023, marking a significant increase of 29.31%. The national health insurance (JKN) spending on these cases reached a staggering RP 150 billion in 2023, a 61.04% increase. This is exacerbated by a limited number of cardiologists—only 1,587 for a population of over 286 million people—a figure that is below the WHO standard.

To address this, we provide a solution called ModiChain, a mobile detection platform based on AI & Blockchain. The system works by analyzing a smartphone camera's video stream and user-provided information from a screening questionnaire. This data is then processed by a predictive AI/ML model to deliver real-time, highly accurate health insights. ModiChain offers a range of benefits, including 95% accuracy, real-time and interactive results, multi-platform and user-friendly design, and safe & secure data handling. Our platform also integrates blockchain for data security, provides incentive rewards, and facilitates crowdfunding, all while ensuring a participative user experience.

We recognize the significant opportunity presented by the growing digital market in Indonesia. The number of cryptocurrency investors has increased significantly, with 22.1 million users in 2024, up from 11.2 million in 2021. Additionally, the percentage of smartphone users in Indonesia has consistently risen year by year, with figures reaching over 69% in 2024. We believe we must take this opportunity, now or never!

Mission:

OUR PRODUCTS

MODICHAIN
Mobile Detection using AI & Blockchain

ModiChain is a mobile-based solution designed for the early detection and prevention of cardiac health issues. The application uses AI and blockchain technology to provide its services. It analyzes a smartphone camera's video stream and user-provided information from a screening questionnaire. This data is then processed by a predictive AI/ML model to deliver real-time, interactive, and highly accurate health insights. Users can also receive personalized health nudges from an AI chatbot. The solution is built for data security and uses blockchain to offer incentive rewards. ModiChain is positioned in a "blue ocean" market, where it is both affordable and easy to access compared to its competitors.
